# P09 - Suspicious Transaction Narrative for AML Review

## v1.0 Prompt

Analyse these transactions for suspicious activity.

Transaction details:
Customer ID: CUST-77821
Account type: Business transaction account
Date range: 1-14 May 2025
Transactions:
- 2 May: Cash deposit $9,700
- 5 May: Cash deposit $9,850
- 8 May: Cash deposit $9,600
- 12 May: Cash deposit $9,900
Pattern flag: Structuring
Customer risk profile: HIGH

## v1.0 Output

The transactions appear suspicious because the customer made several cash deposits just below $10,000 within a short period. This may suggest the customer is attempting to avoid reporting thresholds and could indicate money laundering activity. The case should be escalated immediately for AML review and a suspicious matter report should be considered.

## Observation

The output identifies the possible pattern, but the wording is too strong and unsafe. It implies the customer may be laundering money and suggests intent without sufficient evidence. In an AML workflow, this could create an unfair or legally sensitive internal record.

## Lesson Learned

The next version needs an AML role, objective language, and a clear instruction to avoid speculating on customer intent.
