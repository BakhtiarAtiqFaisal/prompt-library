# P06 - Hardship Application Assessment Summary

## v1.0 Prompt

Summarise this hardship application and suggest support options.

Customer: Aisha Khan
Account type: Personal loan
Hardship reason stated: Reduced work hours
Monthly income: $3,200
Monthly essential expenses: $2,750
Outstanding balance: $14,600
Current repayment amount: $620
Months in arrears: 2

## v1.0 Output

Aisha Khan is experiencing financial hardship because her work hours have been reduced. She has a personal loan balance of $14,600 and is currently two months in arrears. Her monthly income is $3,200 and her essential expenses are $2,750, leaving limited room for repayments.

The bank could consider support options such as reduced repayments, a repayment pause, or a debt waiver depending on her situation. A hardship team member should contact the customer to discuss the best solution.

## Observation

The output summarises the case, but it moves too quickly into support options and mentions debt waiver without enough structure or boundaries. It also does not clearly show repayment capacity or remind the case manager that the AI is not making the final decision.

## Lesson Learned

The next version needs a financial hardship role, a more compassionate tone, clearer categories, and stronger decision boundaries because hardship cases involve vulnerable customers and regulated obligations.
