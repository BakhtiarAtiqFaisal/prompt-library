# P06 - Hardship Application Assessment Summary

## v1.1 Prompt

You are a financial hardship specialist at an Australian retail bank.

Review the following hardship application and produce a short assessment summary for the case manager.

Customer: Aisha Khan
Account type: Personal loan
Hardship reason stated: Reduced work hours
Monthly income: $3,200
Monthly essential expenses: $2,750
Outstanding balance: $14,600
Current repayment amount: $620
Months in arrears: 2

Include:
1. Hardship category: Temporary / Ongoing / Severe
2. Possible support options
3. Key concerns for the case manager
4. Whether referral to financial counselling may be useful

Use a compassionate and professional tone.

## v1.1 Output

### 1. Hardship Category: Temporary

The hardship appears temporary because the stated reason is reduced work hours. Further information is needed to confirm whether the income reduction is short-term or ongoing.

### 2. Possible Support Options

- Reduced repayments for a short period
- Temporary repayment pause
- Extended repayment term if appropriate

### 3. Key Concerns for the Case Manager

- The customer is already two months in arrears.
- Essential expenses leave limited available income.
- The current repayment amount may not be affordable without adjustment.

### 4. Financial Counselling Referral

A referral may be useful if the reduced work hours are ongoing or if the customer has other debts.

## Observation

The output is more respectful and useful than v1.0 because it adds a hardship role, category and case-manager focus. However, it still does not calculate estimated surplus income clearly and does not explicitly state that the AI must not make the final hardship decision.

## Lesson Learned

The next version needs a transparent repayment-capacity calculation, bounded assistance options and stronger wording that the case manager must verify all figures and make the final decision.
