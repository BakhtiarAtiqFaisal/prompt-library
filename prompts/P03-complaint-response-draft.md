# P03 - Complaint Response Draft

## v1.0 Prompt

Write a reply to this customer complaint.

Customer complaint used for testing:
Customer name: Daniel Lee
Complaint category: Fees
Complaint summary: Customer disputes a monthly account fee that they were told would be waived.
Urgency: Medium
Regulatory flag: NO
Action taken: Reviewing the fee waiver record and transaction history


## v1.0 Output

Dear Daniel,

Thank you for contacting us. I am very sorry that we charged you a monthly account fee when you were told it would be waived. This was our mistake, and we will refund the fee to your account as soon as possible. We understand how frustrating this must be and apologise for the inconvenience caused.

We will review your account and make sure this does not happen again. Please allow a few business days for the refund to appear. If you have any further questions, please contact us.

Kind regards,  
Customer Support Team

## Observation

The response is polite, but it is risky for a banking complaint workflow. It strongly apologises, says the bank made a mistake, and promises a refund before the issue has been verified. This could create liability and compensation expectations.

## Lesson Learned

The next version needs clearer business context, a customer service role, a professional tone, and a safer structure that acknowledges the issue without admitting fault or promising compensation.
