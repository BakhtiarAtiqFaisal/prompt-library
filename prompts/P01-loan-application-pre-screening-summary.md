# P01 - Loan Application Pre-Screening

## v1.0 Prompt

Review the following loan application and tell me if the applicant should get the loan.

Application details:
Applicant name: Mia Nguyen
Loan type: Personal loan
Loan amount: $18,000
Annual income: $74,000
Employment status: Full-time
Credit score: 724
Existing debts: $9,500 credit card debt

## v1.0 Output

Mia Nguyen appears to be a suitable candidate for a personal loan. She has a full-time job, earns $74,000 annually, and has a good credit score of 724. Her existing debt of $9,500 should be considered, but based on the information provided, she seems likely to manage the loan repayments. The bank should proceed with the application, subject to normal verification checks.

## v1.0 Observation

The output is too general and sounds like a final lending decision. It does not clearly separate eligibility, risk factors, missing information, or next steps. It also does not include enough caution that AI should not make the final credit decision.

## v1.0 Lesson Learned

The prompt needs a clear banking role, structured sections, and stronger wording that the output is only a pre-screening summary, not a final approval decision.
